Chicken Marinade

Description

Irresistbly juicy, chargrilled chicken begins with a well-executed marinade - and this is a perfectly smoky, salty, delicious recipe that gets the job done every time.

This tantalizing blend creates a rich flavor that will make you the star of the cookout. Learn how to create a perfect marinade, and how long it should take to truly take your dishes to the next level.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup olive oil
  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons liquid smoke
  • 2 tablespoons spicy brown mustard
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, liquid smoke, mustard, pepper, and garlic powder together in a large glass or ceramic bowl.
  2. Place chicken in bowl and turn to coat with marinade. Cover and marinate in the fridge for 1-4 hours. For best results, do not marinate longer than 4 hours.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess. Discard the remaining marinade.
  5. Cook chicken until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 6-8 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
